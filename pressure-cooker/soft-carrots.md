# Soft Carrots

## Time

- Prep Time: 2 min
- Cook Time: 15 min
- Total Time: 17 min

## Ingredients

- 1 package baby carrots
- 1 cup water

## Steps

1. Add the water to the pressure cooker.
2. Place the baby carrots in the steamer basket.
3. Turn on the pressure cooker to **low pressure** for 3 minutes.
