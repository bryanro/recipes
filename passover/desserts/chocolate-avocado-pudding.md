# Chocolate Avocado Pudding

## Time

- Prep Time: 10 min
- Cook Time: 30 min
- Total Time: 40 min

## Ingredients

- 2 large avocados
- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/3 cup almond flour
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 pinch cinnamon

## Steps

1. Blend everything together until smooth.
2. Put in fridge for at least 30 minutes.
