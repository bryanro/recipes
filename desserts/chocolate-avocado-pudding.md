# Chocolate Avocado Pudding

## Time

- Prep Time: # min
- Cook Time: # min
- Total Time: # min

## Ingredients

- 2 large avocados (cubed)
- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/3 cup coconut or almond milk
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 pinch cinnamon

## Steps

1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
2. Refrigerate for 30+ minutes.
