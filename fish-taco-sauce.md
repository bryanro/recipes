# Fish Taco Sauce

## Time

- Prep Time: 5 min
- Cook Time: 0 min
- Total Time: 5 min

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/3 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Sriracha to taste

## Steps

1. Whisk together all ingredients.
