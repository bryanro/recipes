# Almond Crusted Chicken

## Time

- Prep Time: 5 min
- Cook Time: 15 min
- Total Time: 20 min

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup roasted, unsalted almonds
- 1 garlic clove
- 1/2 tsp coarse salt
- 1 tsp paprika (optional)
- 2 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 2 organic boneless, skinless chicken breasts, halved (4 pieces total)

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 425.
2. Make a coarse paste with almonds, garlic, salt, paprika and olive oil in a food processor or blender.
3. Rub onto chicken.
4. Roast at 425 degrees in the oven until cooked through, 15 to 18 minutes.
