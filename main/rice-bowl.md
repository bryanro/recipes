# Rice Bowl

## Time

- Prep Time: 10 min
- Cook Time: 15 min
- Total Time: 25 min

## Ingredients

- brown rice
- edamame
- red onion
- peas
- pineapple
- green onion
- fresh seaweed
- beets
- chickpeas
- radish
- egg
- salmon
- ponzu sauce (soy sauce plus citrus)
- [Japanese white sauce](../sauces/japanese-white-sauce.md)

## Steps

1. Cook rice and combine all ingredients. Rice is pressure cooker is 1 cup water for every 1 cup rice.
